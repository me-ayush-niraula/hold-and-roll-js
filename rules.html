How to Play?

  1. 1. Your main goal in this game is to reach 20 points to win.
  2. 2. Getting 1(one) on the dice resets your score.
  3. 3. Player 1 can either roll as long as he/she wants or hold the value to give Player 2 a chance.
  4. 4. Player 2 can similarly either roll or hold the value hoping not to get 1(one) on a dice.
  5. 5. Player who gets score 20 wins.
